About me

It is an interesting world I find myself in, acting between business and IT. I have been engrossed in digital technology since the beginning of the millennium. After my studies in computer science and economics, I worked as a consultant in areas across the tech sphere, mostly in data integration and process digitalisation. In the IT we are all too well aware that we are stuck with technology, but the only thing we want is just something that works. I beliefe that the most the problems in the IT causes in complexity. But if you look at it right, you find a very simple answer for 80% of all problems during creating the most complex IT ecosystems.
